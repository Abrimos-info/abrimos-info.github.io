Lines of work

At Abrimos.info we combine our experience opening data with extensive narrative, research and development capabilities to create reliable and verified databases that promote strategic knowledge.

Our core software, TeseoETL, extracts, structures, analyzes and publishes data on platforms to address the misinformation that plagues democracies around the world. We apply it to these four lines of work:
